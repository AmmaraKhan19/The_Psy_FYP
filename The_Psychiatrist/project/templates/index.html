Application

This application is 90% accurate in identifying the potentially suicidal people. Because twitter is one of the most used social platforms, this application will help in identifying suicidal people on twiiter through their tweets. This application is to provide help to the first responders to a suicidal attempt. To help them identify such persons and provide help timely.
